Users begin by registering for an account on the ShopEZ platform.

After successful registration, they can log in using their email and password credentials.

Once logged in, users can explore the homepage, browse products by category, and view featured products.

Users can search and filter products to find items based on their preferences.

After selecting a product, they can view detailed product information.

Users can add desired products to their cart and manage cart items by updating quantity or removing items.

They can proceed to checkout by providing shipping address and selecting a payment method.

After placing the order, a custom order number is generated for tracking purposes.

Users can track their order status in the profile section under “My Orders.”

Once the product is delivered, users can submit reviews for purchased products.
